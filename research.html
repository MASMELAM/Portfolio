Research

Compound Flood Hazard Modeling in the Knights Research Scholars Program

I am working with a team of 5 undergraduate students to assess compound flood hazards by setting up and running the dynamic flood modeling tool (SFINCS). I am also responsible for relating characteristics of compound events to the degree of resulting floods through MATLAB and ArcGIS. The coastal area that we are focusing on for this study is Philadelphia.

For more details, please refer to the Knight Research Scholars Program website: Compound Flood Hazard Modeling
